About

Needful exists to alleviate poverty by enabling the world to Needful. Established in 2023, Needful, together with its partners, is the largest and most trusted giving platform in India. Needful enables individuals and organizations to raise and donate funds conveniently to any cause they care about, with offerings including crowdfunding, corporate giving, cause marketing, and philanthropy consulting. Needful's community of 2.6M+ donors supports 2,800+ verified nonprofits, serving 15M+ people across the country.
